Privacy Policy

Welding to Recycle Inc — Effective June 1, 2026

Welding to Recycle Inc (“Welding to Recycle,” “WTR,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) builds and sells recycled shipping-container structures, including offices, living units, and related products. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you contact us — including through lead forms on Facebook and Instagram and through our website — and how we use and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a lead form or contact us, we may collect:

  • Contact information — your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Project details you choose to share — such as how you intend to use the unit, your timeline, and the city or area where it would be delivered.
  • Information you provide in messages — anything else you include when you reach out to us.

We collect this information only when you voluntarily provide it.

2. How We Collect It

We collect the information above primarily through:

  • Lead forms on Facebook and Instagram (Meta “Instant Forms”), which you fill out within those apps after seeing one of our ads;
  • Our website, including contact and quote-request forms; and
  • Direct contact, such as when you email or call us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your quote request or inquiry;
  • Contact you about our products, pricing, delivery, and installation;
  • Follow up on your interest and answer your questions; and
  • Improve how we serve and communicate with prospective customers.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only as needed to operate our business, including with:

  • Service providers who help us manage inquiries, communications, and advertising on our behalf;
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. (Facebook and Instagram), when you submit a lead form through their platforms, in which case their processing is also governed by Meta’s own data policies; and
  • Authorities or third parties where required by law.

5. Advertising and Lead Forms

If you submit a lead form through one of our Facebook or Instagram ads, Meta collects and transmits your form responses to us so we can follow up. Your interaction with Meta’s platforms is also subject to Meta’s own terms and privacy policy. You can review how Meta handles your data at Meta’s Privacy Policy.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies, including advertising and analytics tools, to understand how visitors use the site and to measure the performance of our ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings. If we run advertising measurement tools (such as the Meta Pixel) on our website, they help us understand which ads lead to inquiries; they do not collect more contact information than you choose to provide.

7. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, serve you as a customer, and meet our legal and business obligations. When it is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you, or to opt out of further contact. We will respond to reasonable requests as required by applicable law. To make a request, use the contact details below.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our products and communications are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
